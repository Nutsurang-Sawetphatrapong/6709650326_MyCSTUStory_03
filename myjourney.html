My journey

I would like to tell you about my journey in Thammasat University.

Years 1-2: In the first two years, students focus on foundational computer science courses such as Introduction to Programming, Data Structures, Computational Mathematics, and Algorithms. These courses establish essential skills for further studies in computer science. Additionally, students take basic science and technology subjects, which provide important context for understanding computing principles.

Years 3: In the third year, students move on to more advanced topics like System Design and Analysis, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Image Processing, and Computer Networks. These courses deepen the students' understanding of complex computing systems and prepare them for practical applications in research and large-scale projects.

Year 4: In the final year, students choose elective courses that align with their areas of interest, such as Software Development, Data Science, or Networking. They also complete a capstone project or thesis, where they apply all the knowledge and skills gained throughout their studies to solve real-world problems.
